Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Description

This Spaghetti Bolognese is a hearty Italian dish made with a rich and savory meat sauce served over al dente spaghetti. Itâ€™s perfect for a cozy family meal or dinner party.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz spaghetti
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large pan, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  3. Add chopped onion and minced garlic to the pan, cooking until softened.
  4. Stir in diced tomatoes, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Let the sauce simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  5. Serve the sauce over the cooked spaghetti. Garnish with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
