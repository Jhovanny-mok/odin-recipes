Lasagna Recipe

Description

This is a classic lasagna recipe with layers of delicious pasta, savory meat sauce, and plenty of melted cheese. Perfect for family dinners or any special occasion!

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a pan, heat olive oil and cook the ground beef until browned. Add garlic powder, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Stir in marinara sauce and let simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. In a baking dish, spread a layer of meat sauce, followed by a layer of noodles, then a layer of ricotta cheese. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used.
  5. Top with mozzarella cheese and bake for 30-40 minutes, or until bubbly and golden brown on top.
  6. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Enjoy!
